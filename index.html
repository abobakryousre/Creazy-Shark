Fish Frenzy

the game has three level each one is harder than the previous, as long as you eat smaller fish your score increase and become able to eat anther type of fish until you reach the end of the current level. then you will move to next level.

Try not to be eaten by a bigger fish. Collect a lot of sea stars because your lives will increase one for each five sea stars. Eat the speacial fish at level 2 to make all big fishes smaller than you. Try to finish all levels as fast as possible to get our speed badges for every level.
